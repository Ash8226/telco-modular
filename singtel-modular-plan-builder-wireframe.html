Prototyping and Wireframing

Wireframe for Singtel Smart Upgrade Builder

Step wise guide on how to do wireframing

Step 1: Preparation

  • Identify the key screens needed to explain the Modular Plan Builder.
  • Define Praveen’s goal: choose plan modules, phone, and contract clearly.
  • Prioritise desktop, tablet, and mobile layouts for accessibility.

Step 2: Wireframing

  • Create screen blocks for the hero, Mira guide, module library, phone selection, plan builder, and cost summary.
  • Show how drag-and-drop modules move into the plan builder.
  • Place Monthly Cost Summary bottom-right on laptop, stacked on handheld screens.
